Eric Gilmore

Eric Gilmore (2000, Dallas, Texas) is an artist and painter working with mixed media, including acrylic, oil, spray paint, and different types of techniques to bring a unique texture and depth to his work. Located in Los Angeles, California his paintings often explore themes of identity, feelings, anxiety, culture, and the human experience. Focusing on vibrant colors and traditional imagery lots of his work contains many layers and hidden details. Combining elements of neo-expressionism, street art, contemporary, abstract, and traditional tattoo art, to bring a unique look at how he sees the world.
