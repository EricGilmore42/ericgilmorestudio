Eric Gilmore ← Home

Los Angeles-based painter working with mixed media, including acrylic, oil, spray paint, and different types of media to bring a unique texture and depth to his work. His paintings often explore themes of identity, culture, and the human experience, with a focus on vibrant colors and traditional imagery. Combining elements of neo-expressionism, street art, contemporary, abstract, and traditional tattoo art, to bring a unique look at how I see the world.
